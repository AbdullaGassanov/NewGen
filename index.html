    My job and one of my hobbies is Internet technologies and everything connected with them. I love working and learning with new technologies and trends in web development. Some time after, I was engaged in business automation for supermarkets, pharmacies, restaurants, etc. I installed software (Point of Sale, SQL DataBase) and set up equipment (barcode scanner, receipt printers, fiscal registrars), trained staff for work with software. At some point in my career, I wrote simple corporate websites in ASP.Net, but didn't go deep into website building due to my other IT job. For two years now, I have retrained and write sites with in-depth study of the frontend (HTML5, CSS, SCSS, Javascript, ReactJS). I have already developed enough sites, with adaptive and intuitive design. My approach in web-developing is not to load many libraries and use the native features of Grids/Flex-boxes and JS, to ensure that web pages load easily and the site is not heavy(compressed code). Website development has become an exciting adventure for me. And I try to do this job well.
